About Konkan

The coastal beauty of Maharashtra

Nature's Paradise

Konkan is a beautiful coastal region located between the Arabian Sea and the Western Ghats. It is famous for its stunning beaches, coconut trees, seafood, forts, temples, and peaceful villages.

Districts like Ratnagiri, Sindhudurg, Raigad, Palghar, and Thane make Konkan one of the most attractive tourist destinations in Maharashtra.

History of Konkan

A land with rich cultural heritage

Ancient Period

Konkan has been known since the 3rd century CE as Aparanta and was ruled by dynasties like Mauryas and Satavahanas.

Maratha Empire

The Konkan coast played an important role in the Maratha Empire and was protected by many sea forts.

Portuguese Influence

Portuguese culture influenced many coastal regions, especially around Goa and nearby areas.
